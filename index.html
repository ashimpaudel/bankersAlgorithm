Where comfort meets productivity

Since opening our doors in 2018, Nest's Boys Hostel has become a second home for students pursuing their dreams in Kathmandu. Our caretakers live on-site, our kitchen serves homely Nepali and continental sets, and every corner is designed to keep you focused and relaxed.

We sit along the bustling Maitidevi-Tinkune corridor with easy microbus access to Patan, Baneshwor, and the Ring Road. Parents appreciate our transparent updates while students love the welcoming environment that inspires focus, friendship, and a true sense of community.
