Where comfort meets productivity

Since opening our doors in 2018, Nest's Boys Hostel has become a second home for students pursuing their dreams in Kathmandu. Our dedicated caretakers, nutritious kitchen, and thoughtfully curated spaces ensure every resident feels supported.

We are located within walking distance of major colleges, public transport, and local amenities, making daily routines effortless. Parents trust us for our transparency while students love the welcoming environment that inspires focus and friendship.
